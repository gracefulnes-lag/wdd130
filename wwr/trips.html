Our Rafting Trips

Ready to book your adventure? Contact us to reserve your rafting trip and start creating unforgettable memories!

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Advanced

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Experience the thrill of one of the most challenging rapids in the region. "The Devil's Teeth" is perfect for experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline rush. Navigate through Class IV and V rapids with our expert guides who know every turn and twist of this exciting course.

Price: $149 per person

Big Mallard

Difficulty: Intermediate

Duration: Half Day (4 hours)

This popular trip combines excitement with accessibility. Big Mallard offers Class III rapids that provide thrilling moments without excessive danger. Perfect for families and groups with mixed skill levels, you'll experience genuine adventure in a beautiful canyon setting.

Price: $99 per person

Salmon River

Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate

Duration: Full Day (7 hours)

Ideal for first-time rafters and families, the Salmon River offers a mix of gentle floats and exciting Class II-III rapids. Enjoy scenic views, wildlife spotting opportunities, and time to appreciate the natural beauty while getting wet and having fun.

Price: $125 per person
